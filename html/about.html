About us

At RainyDays, our mission is to equip passionate explorers with raincoats that stand up to any weather condition, allowing them to embrace the outdoors without hesitation. Whether you're hiking up a mountain trail, exploring rugged coastlines, or simply enjoying the beauty of nature, we believe that the right gear can make all the difference. Our journey began with a challenge—creating raincoats that perform in the harshest conditions without compromising on quality or affordability. From relentless rains to gusty winds, we designed our products to thrive in the elements. The result? Three outstanding raincoat lines: Bergen, Trondheim, and Tromsø, each named after some of Norway’s most weather-resilient cities. We’re committed to delivering high-performance outerwear at a price that makes outdoor adventure accessible to everyone. Quality, durability, and comfort are at the core of what we do, ensuring that you’re ready for whatever nature throws your way. Join us on our journey to explore the world—rain or shine.
